About me

Hi, I’m Vidhi Patra — a Computer Science major at Georgia Tech with a strong interest in AI, machine learning, and data-driven systems. My work bridges technical rigor with real-world impact, spanning algorithmic trading, ethical AI governance, and cancer detection research. From building immersive AR learning platforms to presenting research at national conferences, I thrive on learning by doing. My projects and internships reflect my passion for applying CS in meaningful, interdisciplinary ways — whether that’s predicting natural disasters, optimizing trading strategies, or building tools that connect investors with startups.
